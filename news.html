International Training Course in Azerbaijan-I

1st training was focusing on the basics of entrepreneurship and the role of innovation. It answered questions like why to start a new business, what are the funding possibilities, what are the main characteristics of an entrepreneur. It covered the best and the worst practices of entrepreneurship and how one can turn his/her idea into a reality to be worked on for a long time. 1st training also focused on building the entrepreneurial mindset in the participants; helped them to develop soft skills like decision making, recognizing and using talent, creativity, innovation, persistence, flexibility, and resilience. In the 1st trainings a support system was established through practical implementation and ended with the evaluation of the process.

International Training Course in Azerbaijan-II

2nd training focused on the skills that are needed to keep the business going. Meaning, it included topics like how to ensure the sustainability of the project, how to handle and learn failure, how to celebrate your wins and harvest the results. The 2nd training also focused on the preparation for the upcoming training for trainers in the field of entrepreneurship.

Local Workshop in Azerbaijan

Local Workshop in Azerbaijan started with the explanation of who a trainer is and why it is necessary to have the trainers with the right understanding and knowledge. The training also covered the importance of the learning process and how one can use himself/herself as a learning.

Local Workshop in Belarus

Local Workshop in Belarus was focused on the evaluation of the local situation and how can this kind of trainings can be implemented to meet the need of local people.

Local Workshop in Georgia

Local Workshop in Georgia is introduced the methods and tools that are used in the field of entrepreneurial education, explained observation and feedback practice.
